My name is David Forystek. I am currently a junior at the University of Michigan School of Information studying to earn a Bachelor of Science in Information degree and I have earned an Associates of Applied Science degree as a Computer Support Technician from Schoolcraft College. I am currently employed at the University of Michigan School of Nursing as the Technology Coordiator for the I-CONECT research study in conjunction with Oregon Health and Science University (OHSU) where I set up and deploy chromebooks for study participants to use for video chats over the course of the study.

My professional goal is to have a career in the field of cybersecurity. I have always had a fascination with network and computer security and I hope to help people make sure their data is private and secured. I have always had a personal interest in computer networking and cybersecurity and make sure I always use best practices regards to data privacy.
